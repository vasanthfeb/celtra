Introducing Creative Showcase: How to Create Impactful Presentations of Your Ad Products to Engage And Impress

Celtra Last updated: September 24, 2024
4 min read

Table of Contents

Discover the new Creative Showcase for Creative Enablement—a powerful, customizable tool that allows you to build a compelling portfolio of your best ad experiences and lets you tell your story in your own way. 

Whether it’s new ad products or successful campaigns, this easy-to-use tool lets you enhance your sales presentations and impress advertisers by presenting your best work in a simple and streamlined way. 

With Celtra’s Creative Showcase, you can:

  • Copy creatives from any past campaign or ad product folder within your account.
  • Customize your portfolio by personalizing it to your own use case by setting logos, uploading thumbnails of your experiences, adding descriptions, and creating custom classifications for easier filtering.
  • Share your portfolio with a simple link, giving prospects a tailored, easy-to-navigate gallery that highlights the best of your creative capabilities.

 

More than an ad product portfolio tool: An end-to-end solution

Creative Showcase is not just a portfolio tool—it’s a comprehensive solution that simplifies your entire pre-sales process. Here’s how:

  • Saves time: Reduce the effort needed to create and maintain a high-impact portfolio. Creative Showcase integrates seamlessly with the Celtra platform, allowing you to create, review, manage traffic, and analyze all in one place.
  • Enhances presentations: With customizable galleries, you can tailor your portfolio to fit the specific needs and interests of each prospect, making your presentations more engaging and relevant.
  • Boosts sales potential: By showcasing your best work in an easily accessible and visually appealing format, you increase the likelihood of converting prospects into clients.
