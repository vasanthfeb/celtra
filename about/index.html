Our story

2006 Co-founders Miha and Maja Mikek along with Matevž Klanjšek incubate Celtra at Babson College.
2007 The first product, FunkyCall, is launched.
2008 Celtra Marketing Platform follows, transforming campaign management in mobile marketing.
2010 The new AdCreator propels Celtra into the creative technology space.
2011–2016 Celtra secures a series of funding rounds and becomes the leading creative platform for mobile rich media ads with over 400 global clients.
2017 The first global brand direct partnership starts with Unilever.
2020 The Creative Automation platform is launched as a pioneer in its category, enabling advertisers to execute complex global campaigns faster and boost ad performance.
2021 A new chapter of growth begins with a successful majority stake acquisition by STG Partners.
2023 The second-generation Creative Automation platform is introduced, with AI capabilities integrated across Celtra’s solutions.
2024 onward Continuous innovation and growth.

Miha Mikek

Founder & CEO

Miha Mikek

Founder & CEO

Miha Mikek is the Founder and CEO of Celtra. Born in Slovenia, he started several companies before founding Celtra in 2006 while completing his graduate studies at Babson College. Under his leadership, Celtra pioneered a self-service platform that made rich media ads on mobile truly scalable. His work fueled exponential growth from 2011 to 2016, establishing Celtra as a global leader in enabling media companies, advertising platforms, service providers, and advertisers. Miha led the company through multiple rounds of venture capital financing, culminating in a successful majority stake acquisition by STG, where he partnered with the new investors. He continues to drive Celtra’s innovation and investment in market-first solutions like Creative Automation—helping the world’s largest advertisers scale creative and streamline creative and media workflows to help companies achieve better advertising outcomes.

Outside the office
Beyond Celtra, Miha is an advisor to tech founders, an active board member, and an investor in technology companies. After 16 years in Boston, he now lives in Geneva, Switzerland, with his wife, Maja (Celtra’s co-founder and CFO), and their children, Sofia and Lev. He finds inspiration in outdoor sports, family adventures, and the pursuit of bold new ideas.
