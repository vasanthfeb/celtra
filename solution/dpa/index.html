Frequently asked questions

What are dynamic product ads? 

Dynamic product ads are an ad format that automatically displays the most relevant items from your product catalog to the viewer based on their interests and other intent signals. With DPA campaigns, you can promote your entire product catalog across channels without creating thousands of ads for each item.

Are Celtra Dynamic Product Ads a self-serve solution?

Yes, Celtra offers an easy-to-use cloud-based software platform where teams can independently produce, review, and distribute countless enriched catalog ads across multiple formats, sizes, and channels. Upon demand, Celtra Services can be leveraged for assistance. Book a demo to find out how to use Celtra Dynamic Product Ads to produce high-performing e-commerce ads easily.

Does Celtra support Facebook dynamic product ads? 

Yes. Besides Meta Advantage+ catalog ads, Celtra’s solution also supports multiple other platforms, including Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok, Snapchat, and X.

How much do dynamic product ads cost at Celtra?

The price of Celtra’s Dynamic Product Ads solution depends on your use case and the number of catalogs you want to run on the platform. Reach out, and our team will prepare a personalized quote for your needs.
