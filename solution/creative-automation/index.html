Frequently asked questions

What is Creative Automation?

Celtra’s Creative Automation platform is a cloud-based software solution that helps marketing, creative and media teams accelerate digital ad production, distribution, and impact.

What is the cost of using the Creative Automation software?

The price of Celtra’s Creative Automation platform depends on your use case and the number of seats you need based on how many people you expect to use the platform. Get in touch and our team will work with you to prepare a personalized quote.

Is Creative Automation the right tool for me?

Ever struggled with slow, outdated processes? Looking to boost your ad performance? By leveraging the power of automation and AI, Creative Automation can help your team move faster and achieve more with fewer resources. If you’re still not sure if our cloud-based software solution is the right fit for you, book a meeting with our team.

How can I get started with Creative Automation?

Book a meeting with our team to discuss your goals. We’ll prepare a contract tailored to your needs and once signed, we can kick off the onboarding process. Don’t worry – our services team will be there to guide you every step of the way as you familiarize yourself with our easy-to-use Creative Automation platform.
